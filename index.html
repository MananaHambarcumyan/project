Sweet Potato and Kale Bowl

Learn different ways to cook potatoes

Ingredients

For the sweet potato

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 head cauliflower
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup milk

For the kale

  • 1 tablespoon bacon fat
  • 3 cups chopped kale
  • 3 cloves garlic minced

Instructions

  1. Place potatoes into a pot with a steamer insert and enough water to reach the bottom of the potatoes.
  2. Place a lid on top and turn the heat to medium-high. The steam gets hotter than boiling in water, allowing the potatoes to cook faster.
  3. After about 15-20 minutes the potatoes should be fork-tender and ready for mashed potatoes.
